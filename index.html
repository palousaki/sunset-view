About the Space

A place to breathe in Greece

Brand new and thoughtfully appointed, Sunset View is a quiet retreat on the second floor of a two-storey building in Paralia Ofryniou - steps from the sea, yet close to the heart of the village.

The apartment comfortably sleeps up to five guests, making it perfect for families or small groups seeking an authentic Greek seaside escape. Wake up to the shimmering Aegean, enjoy your morning coffee on the terrace, and let the rhythm of the sea set the pace for your day.
